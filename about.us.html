About Kira Clinic

Kira Clinic was founded with a simple yet powerful mission: to provide accessible, high-quality healthcare services to our community in Nyagatare and beyond. We believe in putting patients first by combining expert care with compassion and respect.

Our Vision

To become a leading healthcare provider in Rwanda, known for innovation, integrity, and a people-centered approach to health and wellness.

Our Mission

We are committed to offering affordable, reliable, and holistic healthcare services that meet the needs of individuals and families across all age groups.
