Our Medical Services

General Consultation

Personalized health assessments and guidance from our experienced general practitioners for a wide range of medical concerns. Our doctors provide thorough examinations and tailored treatment plans.

Laboratory Testing

Comprehensive lab services including blood tests, urine analysis, and more using modern diagnostic technology. Fast and accurate results to support your diagnosis and treatment.

Vaccination Programs

Routine immunizations for children and adults to protect against infectious diseases and maintain public health. We follow national vaccination schedules and offer travel vaccines.

Dental Care

Preventive, cosmetic, and surgical dental services including cleanings, fillings, extractions, and dental education. Our dental team focuses on pain-free treatments and oral health education.

Maternity & Prenatal Care

Support for expectant mothers including prenatal checkups, ultrasound scans, and maternal health education. We provide compassionate care throughout pregnancy and postpartum.

Chronic Disease Management

Ongoing care and monitoring for conditions such as diabetes, hypertension, and asthma to improve quality of life. Our team helps patients manage long-term conditions effectively.

Child Health Services

Pediatric services focused on the health and development of infants, children, and adolescents. We provide well-child visits, immunizations, and treatment for childhood illnesses.

Nutrition Counseling

Advice and planning for balanced diets, weight management, and nutrition therapy for health conditions. Our nutritionists create personalized meal plans for optimal health.

Health Education

Community outreach programs to raise awareness about hygiene, preventive care, and healthy lifestyles. We conduct workshops and seminars on various health topics.

Meet Our Specialists

Dr. Aline Mugisha

Chief Medical Officer & General Practitioner

With over 15 years of experience in family medicine, Dr. Mugisha leads our clinical team with expertise in preventive care and chronic disease management. She completed her medical training at the University of Rwanda and specializes in community health initiatives.

Dr. Jean Bosco

Laboratory Specialist

Dr. Bosco brings 10 years of laboratory diagnostics experience to Kira Clinic. With advanced training in clinical pathology from Makerere University, he ensures accurate test results and implements quality control measures in our lab.

Dr. Grace Niyonsaba

Pediatrician & Vaccination Specialist

Dr. Niyonsaba specializes in child health and immunization programs. After completing her pediatrics residency at King Faisal Hospital, she has dedicated her career to improving child health outcomes in rural communities.
